Tech Heroes

Dishanta Kpatrick

Dishanta Kpatrick's journey into tech is a testament to the power of self-learning, resilience, and community support. From teaching herself to code while working various jobs to completing a full-stack bootcamp and eventually landing a role as a Full Stack Engineer at Intuit Mailchimp, Dishanta’s story highlights the importance of persistence in overcoming barriers. Her experience underscores the value of non-traditional paths, apprenticeship opportunities, and mentorship in tech, offering a powerful message to aspiring engineers: with passion and determination, success in tech is attainable, no matter where you start.

Melanie Perkins

Melanie Perkins, an Australian technology entrepreneur, is the co-founder and CEO of Canva, a graphic design platform that has transformed the way people create visual content. Born in Perth, Australia, Perkins began her entrepreneurial journey at 19 and co-founded Canva in 2012 with the vision of democratizing design and making it accessible to everyone. Under her leadership, Canva has grown into a multi-billion-dollar company used by millions globally. Known for her innovative business approach and dedication to fostering a positive company culture, Perkins' success serves as an inspiration to aspiring tech entrepreneurs.

Emmanuela Ilok

Emmanuela Ilok from Nigeria has earned full scholarships to several prestigious universities, including MIT, Stanford, Yale, Princeton, UPenn, and Columbia, due to her exceptional academic and technological achievements. Her journey began with top performance in the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) in Nigeria, where she was recognized by the British Council. Ilok went on to develop software using Machine Learning to detect breast cancer with 91% accuracy, showcasing her talent in tech innovation. At just 16, she won the prestigious RISE program with her project “CodEd,” which aimed to improve computer science education in Nigerian public schools through teacher training and student internships. Her accomplishments have been praised by education advocate Alex Onyia, and she now serves as an inspiration for Nigerian students, sharing her experience and offering guidance on pursuing global educational opportunities.

Fei-Fei Li

Fei-Fei Li, a leading expert in artificial intelligence, has navigated the unique challenges of being both a woman and a person of color in a field historically dominated by white men. She has been a vocal advocate for increasing diversity in AI, emphasizing how biased datasets can lead to systems that disadvantage marginalized groups. Through her work with AI4ALL, Li actively addresses these issues by fostering greater inclusion in tech, focusing on empowering young women and students of color with access to AI education. Her initiatives highlight the importance of ensuring diverse voices contribute to shaping the future of AI, addressing the compounded barriers of race, gender, and class.
